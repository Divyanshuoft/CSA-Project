About UTSC Co-op Students Association

Welcome to the UTSC Co-op Students Association! We are a dynamic and inclusive organization that represents the interests of co-op students at the University of Toronto Scarborough. Our mission is to empower co-op students by fostering a strong sense of community, providing valuable resources, and creating opportunities for personal and professional growth.

Who We Are

At the heart of our association are the co-op students who make up our vibrant and diverse community. We are committed to ensuring that your co-op experience is enriching and successful. Whether you're an undergraduate or a graduate co-op student, we're here to support you throughout your journey.

Our Goals

  • Community Building: We believe in the power of a strong, connected co-op community. We organize various social and networking events throughout the year to help you build meaningful relationships with your peers.
  • Professional Development: Your co-op experience is a valuable stepping stone to your future career. We offer workshops, seminars, and resources to enhance your professional skills and assist you in securing rewarding co-op placements.
  • Advocacy: We advocate for co-op students' rights and needs, both within the university and in the broader community. Your voice matters, and we're here to make sure it's heard.
  • Support: The challenges of co-op life can sometimes be overwhelming. We provide a support system to help you navigate your co-op journey successfully. Whether you need academic assistance or just a friendly face to talk to, we're here for you.

Get Involved

The UTSC Co-op Students Association is run by co-op students, for co-op students. We encourage you to get involved and make the most of your co-op experience. You can join our executive team, participate in events, or volunteer your time to help create a more vibrant co-op community.

Contact Us

Have questions or suggestions? We'd love to hear from you! You can reach out to us through our contact page or connect with us on social media.
